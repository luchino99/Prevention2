Labs

The cardio-nephro-metabolic workflow requires a core panel of 10 lab values to produce a defensible stratification. Fields marked * are required; the remaining fields are supporting data (staging, passthrough). Submissions missing any required lab will be rejected at validation.

If ACR is not provided but both urine albumin (mg/L) and urine creatinine (mg/dL) are, the backend will derive ACR automatically (mg/g). A directly entered ACR always takes precedence.

Clinical context

Lifestyle — Physical activity (WHO / GPAQ)

Split the weekly activity into moderate (e.g. brisk walking, leisurely cycling) and vigorous (e.g. running, fast cycling). The engine computes MET-minutes using the WHO/GPAQ convention (moderate ≈ 4 METs, vigorous ≈ 8 METs) and evaluates the ≥600 MET-min/week target. Sedentary hours are captured independently, because prolonged sitting is an independent CV risk factor (ESC 2021 §3).

PREDIMED MEDAS — Mediterranean diet adherence (optional, 14 items)

Validated Mediterranean diet adherence questionnaire (Estruch et al., PREDIMED trial). Enable the questionnaire to record all 14 yes/no items — partial responses are not submitted because they would bias the adherence band. When disabled, the completeness checker will report PREDIMED_INCOMPLETE as a low-priority data-collection gap.
